## Communication

The ReasonML community is well known for its helpful and reliably active members in
the [Discord chat](https://discord.gg/reasonml). Don't be afraid to join in and
ask questions, especially if you are just starting out! 

**Our full list of channels:**

- [Discord](https://discord.gg/reasonml)
- [Forum](https://reasonml.chat)
- [Twitter](https://twitter.com/reasonml)
- [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/reasonml/)
- [Stack Overflow](http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/reason)
- [DEV](https://dev.to/t/reason)

> **Note:** We stand for inclusivity and good behavior. Please read our [Code of
Conduct](/community/code-of-conduct) and help us keeping our community healthy and
friendly!

## GitHub

### Organizations

- [reasonml](https://github.com/reasonml): The official organization for core Reason infrastructure maintained by Facebook
- [reasonml-community](https://github.com/reasonml-community): The official
organization for important tools & libraries maintained by the community.
- [reasonml-editor](https://github.com/reasonml-editor): Our organization for all your Reason editor plugin needs
- [reasonml-labs](https://github.com/reasonml-labs): This is a community based
organization for useful tools in the incubator phase. We try to keep things in
the experimental phase in the beginning until they are stable enough to be
moved into the `reasonml-community` organization. 

### Core Projects

- [ReasonML Syntax](https://github.com/facebook/reason)
- [BuckleScript](https://github.com/bucklescript/bucklescript)
- [ReasonReact](https://github.com/reasonml/reason-react)
