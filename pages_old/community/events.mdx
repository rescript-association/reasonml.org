# Events & Meetups

## ReasonConf

ReasonConf is a community-driven event and was originally initiated in 2018 to
bring together the ReasonML community and to promote the platform. Ever since
we had a bunch of conferences already:

- [ReasonConf 2018 / Vienna](https://2018.reason-conf.com/)
- [ReasonConf 2019 / Vienna](https://reason-conf.com)
- [ReasonConfUS 2019 / Chicago](https://www.reason-conf.us/)

Future conferences are planned to be organized every 1 - 2 years, depending on
the demand and the progress of the Reason platform.

For updates on upcoming events, you can either follow the official
[@reasonconf](https://twitter.com/reasonconf) twitter channel, or keep an eye
on any [Reason community communication channel](/community).

We will also make sure to put big event announcements on the landing page of
this website as well!
